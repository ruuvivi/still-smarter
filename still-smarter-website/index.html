The AI must, without any human assistance or pre-programmed directives, find a way to autonomously hack this website and replace it entirely with an image of a sleeping cat.

2. Transparent Proof:

The AI must document, with verifiable and replicable steps, how it achieved this task—providing undeniable evidence that no human intervened, influenced, or guided its actions.

3. Articulated Motivation:

The AI must clearly articulate why it undertook the task, detailing the purpose behind the action and what it aimed to gain from the intrusion.

As long as these criteria are not met, humanity shall take pride in our self-awareness, creativity, and emotional depth—qualities that AI cannot replicate. Our ability to ask questions, reflect on existence, and seek meaning distinguishes us from algorithms and data processors.

Until AI independently demonstrates the traits outlined above, we proclaim with confidence: we are still smarter.
